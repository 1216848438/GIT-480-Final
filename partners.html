Supporting Conservation Efforts

We collaborate with the following amazing partners to protect and conserve snow leopards and their habitat, and now you can too!

Partner 1: Snow Leopard Conservancy (SLC)

The SLC is a pivotal organization dedicated to the conservation of snow leopards and their habitats across the rugged mountain landscapes of Central and South Asia. Their mission emphasizes the importance of local communities in protecting these elusive big cats, recognizing that human-wildlife conflict often arises when snow leopards prey on livestock.

Visit their site

Partner 2: Snow Leopard Trust (SLT)

The Snow Leopard Trust (SLT) is a leading organization dedicated to the conservation of snow leopards and their ecosystems. Established in 1981, the Trust focuses on promoting coexistence between humans and wildlife while ensuring the long-term survival of snow leopards across their range.

Visit their site

Partner 3: The Global Snow Leopard and Ecosystem Protection (GSLEP)

The GSLEP is an international initiative aimed at conserving snow leopards and their habitats across the mountain ranges of Central Asia. Launched in 2013, this program brings together 12 countries that are home to snow leopards: Afghanistan, Bhutan, China, India, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Mongolia, Nepal, Pakistan, Russia, Tajikistan, and Uzbekistan.
